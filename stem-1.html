What is STEM/STEAM?

STEAM is the acronym for science, technology, engineering, arts, and mathematics.

Why is STEM/STEAM education important?

STEM education is integrated in our daily life. It is important because it allow students to be exposed to various genres in science; promotes technology, innovation and creativity within a world that is constantly changing. Children are able to make connections between school, home, their local community, different cities, states, and internationally. Solving and improving worldwide events through science, technology, engineering, and mathematics.
