WHO WE ARE We are a mobile non-profit, 501c3 organization who provide services in various communities. Exposing real life situations to our youth, encourages them to lead, assist, and conquer what it means to become a lifelong, productive, problem solver within any setting. Providing children with opportunities to interact with diverse individuals who are less fortunate, will allow them to become more compassionate about the lives of other people. Not only do we believe in the philanthropy of giving back, we also react to needs as they arise, whether it is in our own community or in communities that cross our path.
